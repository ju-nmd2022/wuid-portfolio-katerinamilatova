Escape Card Game

In the 2020/2021 school year, my classmates and I started a student company called Jifunze as part of JA Czech's educational program for high school students. Our company came up with its own product - "Escape Card Game ČAROLES" designed for children aged 6-9.

As the "chief illustrator and creative" I was responsible for company name, logo, photos and promo video, but mainly for the most of the task cards.
